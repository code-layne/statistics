A state DMV claims 50\% of 16-year-olds pass the driving test on the first attempt.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item $H_0$: \ans{$p = 0.50$} \quad $H_a$: \ans{$p < 0.50$}

  \item \ansline{Random (SRS stated --- met); Large Counts: $90(0.50)=45\ge10$, $90(0.50)=45\ge10$ --- met; 10\% met.}

  \item $\hat p = $ \ans{$40/90\approx0.444$} \quad $z = \dfrac{0.444-0.50}{\sqrt{0.50(0.50)/90}} \approx $ \ans{$-1.06$}

  \item $p$-value $= P(Z\le-1.06) \approx$ \ans{$0.1446$}

  \item $p$-value \ans{$>$} $\alpha$. Decision: \ans{Fail to reject $H_0$.}
\end{enumerate}
