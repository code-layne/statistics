Weekday: 24/320. Weekend: 22/200.

\textit{State:} \ansline{$p_W$ = true proportion of weekday discharges readmitted within 30 days; $p_E$ = same for weekend discharges.\\
$H_0: p_W = p_E$; $H_a: p_W < p_E$ (testing whether weekday rate is lower). One-sided left.}

\textit{Plan:} $\hat p_W = 24/320 = \ans{0.075}$; $\hat p_E = 22/200 = \ans{0.110}$; \quad
$\hat p_c = (24+22)/(320+200) = 46/520 = \ans{0.0885}$

\textbf{Random:} \ansline{Records from random samples of weekday and weekend discharges --- assumed.}\\
\textbf{Large Counts (using $\hat p_c \approx 0.0885$):}\\
$320(0.0885)=28.3$, $320(0.9115)=291.7$, $200(0.0885)=17.7$, $200(0.9115)=182.3$ --- \ans{all $\ge10$. \checkmark}\\
\textbf{10\%:} \ansline{Assumed both samples $\le10\%$ of all discharges in each day group.}
