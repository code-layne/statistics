A hospital administrator wants to test whether the proportion of patients who are readmitted
within 30 days is lower for patients discharged on weekdays than on weekends.
Weekday sample: 24 readmissions in 320 discharges.
Weekend sample: 22 readmissions in 200 discharges.

Complete the \textbf{State} and \textbf{Plan} steps.

\textit{State:} Define $p_W$ and $p_E$, and write $H_0$ and $H_a$.
\writelines{2}

\textit{Plan:} Compute $\hat p_W$, $\hat p_E$, and $\hat p_c$.
\writelines{2}

Check conditions (Random, Large Counts, 10\%).
\writelines{4}
