\textbf{Spiral: Completing the Test Setup.}
County 1: 144/360. County 2: 112/320. $\hat p_c \approx 0.376$. $\alpha=0.05$.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item $\hat p_1 = 144/360 = \ans{0.400}$; \quad $\hat p_2 = 112/320 = \ans{0.350}$.

  \item $SE = \sqrt{0.376(0.624)(1/360+1/320)} = \sqrt{0.234624 \times 0.005903} = \sqrt{0.001385} \approx \ans{0.0372}$

  \item $z = (0.400 - 0.350)/0.0372 = 0.050/0.0372 \approx \ans{1.34}$

  \item \ansline{For a two-sided test: $p$-value $= 2 \times P(Z > 1.34) \approx 2(0.0901) = 0.180$. Since $0.180 > 0.05$, fail to reject $H_0$.}
\end{enumerate}
