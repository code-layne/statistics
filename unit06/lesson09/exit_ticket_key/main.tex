A nutritionist studied whether the proportion of adults who eat breakfast daily differs between
two regions. A 95\% confidence interval for $p_{\text{East}} - p_{\text{West}}$ is
$(-0.14, -0.02)$.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \ansline{We are 95\% confident that the true difference ($p_{\text{East}} - p_{\text{West}}$) in the proportions of Eastern and Western region adults who eat breakfast daily is between $-0.14$ and $-0.02$.}

  \item Contains 0? \ans{No}

  \item \ansline{Yes. Since the entire interval $(-0.14, -0.02)$ is negative and does not contain 0, there is convincing evidence that the proportions differ. Because $p_{\text{East}} - p_{\text{West}}$ is negative, the Western region has a higher daily breakfast rate.}
\end{enumerate}
