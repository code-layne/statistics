A nutritionist studied whether the proportion of adults who eat breakfast daily differs between
two regions. A 95\% confidence interval for $p_{\text{East}} - p_{\text{West}}$ is
$(-0.14, -0.02)$.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Interpret this interval in context. \writelines{2}

  \item Does the interval contain 0? \blank{1.5cm}

  \item Does the interval provide convincing evidence that the proportions differ?
        If so, which region has the higher breakfast rate? Justify your answer.
        \writelines{2}
\end{enumerate}
