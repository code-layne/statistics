\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em, itemsep=5pt]
  \item I can interpret a confidence interval for the difference of two population means
        in context, referencing both populations and the direction of subtraction.
        \textit{(UNC-4.Z, Skill 4.B)}
  \item I can use a confidence interval for $\mu_1 - \mu_2$ to justify or refute a claim,
        by determining whether 0 is inside or outside the interval.
        \textit{(UNC-4.AA, Skill 4.D)}
  \item I can explain how changing sample sizes affects the width of a confidence interval
        for the difference of two means.
        \textit{(UNC-4.AB, Skill 4.A)}
\end{enumerate}
