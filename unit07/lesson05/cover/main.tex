\begin{learningtargetbox}
\small
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em, itemsep=5pt]
  \item I can calculate the $t$-test statistic $t = \dfrac{\bar x - \mu_0}{s/\sqrt{n}}$
        for a one-sample $t$-test or matched-pairs $t$-test and find the associated $p$-value.
        \textit{(VAR-7.E, Skill 3.E)}
  \item I can interpret the $p$-value of a significance test for a population mean in context,
        recognizing that it is computed assuming $H_0$ is true.
        \textit{(DAT-3.E, Skill 4.B)}
  \item I can make and justify a formal conclusion by comparing the $p$-value to $\alpha$,
        restating the decision in the context of the research question.
        \textit{(DAT-3.F, Skill 4.E)}
\end{enumerate}
\end{learningtargetbox}
