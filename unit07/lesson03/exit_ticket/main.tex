A 95\% confidence interval for the mean commute time for city workers is
$(24.3,\ 31.7)$ minutes, based on a random sample.

\begin{enumerate}

  \item Write a correct interpretation of this interval.

        \writelines{2}

  \item The city transportation office claims the mean commute time is 22 minutes.
        Does the interval provide convincing evidence against this claim?
        Justify your answer by referencing the interval.

        \writelines{2}

  \item If the sample size were quadrupled (same $\bar x$ and $s$), describe the
        effect on the margin of error. Be specific about the factor.

        \writelines{2}

\end{enumerate}
