The weights of full-grown dogs of a certain breed are approximately normally distributed
with $\mu = 55$ lbs and $\sigma = 7$ lbs. A veterinarian weighs a random sample of
$n = 25$ dogs.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Check all three conditions for using the normal model for $\bar{x}$.
        \writelines{3}

  \item Find $\mu_{\bar{x}}$ and $\sigma_{\bar{x}}$.
        \vspace{0.5in}

  \item Find $P(\bar{x} > 57)$.
        \vspace{0.8in}
\end{enumerate}
