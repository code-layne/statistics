SAT scores are approximately normally distributed with $\mu = 1060$ and $\sigma = 195$.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Find the probability that a randomly selected student scored \emph{above} 1300.
        Show a sketch, a z-score calculation, and your final answer.

        \vspace{1.5in}

  \item Find the SAT score at the \textbf{75th percentile}. Show your work.

        \vspace{1.2in}

  \item Is it appropriate to use the normal model for SAT scores? Give one reason to
        support your answer.

        \writelines{2}
\end{enumerate}
