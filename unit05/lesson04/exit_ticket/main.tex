\begin{enumerate}
  \item A quality control inspector measures the \emph{maximum} diameter in each random
        sample of 20 ball bearings. After many samples, the average of the sample maxima
        is consistently higher than the true population maximum. Is the sample maximum
        a biased or unbiased estimator? In which direction?
        \writelines{2}

  \item A population has $\mu = 50$ and $\sigma = 16$. Compare $\sigma_{\bar{x}}$ for
        $n = 16$ and $n = 64$. What happens to the variability when $n$ is quadrupled?
        \vspace{0.7in}

  \item Name two statistics that are unbiased estimators of their corresponding
        population parameters.
        \writelines{1}
\end{enumerate}
