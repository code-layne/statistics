usage level (\textbf{Low / Moderate / High}) and their GPA category
(\textbf{GPA $<$ 3.0} / \textbf{GPA $\ge$ 3.0}).

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{@{} l c c c @{}}
\hline
                 & \textbf{GPA $<$ 3.0} & \textbf{GPA $\ge$ 3.0} & \textbf{Row Total} \\
\hline
