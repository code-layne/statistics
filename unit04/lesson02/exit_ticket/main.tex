\begin{scenariobox}[Context]{sky}
\small
A bag contains 3 red marbles and 7 blue marbles. A student draws one marble,
records the color, and \textbf{replaces} it. She repeats this process 10 times
and draws red on 4 of the 10 trials.
\end{scenariobox}

\vspace{0.1in}

\begin{practicebox}
\small
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em, itemsep=10pt]
  \item What is the student's \textbf{empirical probability} of drawing red?
        Show your calculation.
        \vspace{16pt}

  \item What is the \textbf{true probability} of drawing red from this bag?
        Show your calculation.
        \vspace{16pt}

  \item The law of large numbers states that \ldots{}\\
        \writeline\writeline

  \item The student decides to repeat the simulation 90 more times (100 total trials).
        Based on the law of large numbers, would you expect her empirical probability
        to be closer to the true probability after 100 trials than after 10? Explain.\\
        \writeline\writeline\writeline
\end{enumerate}
\end{practicebox}
