\begin{notesbox}{The BINS Conditions for a Binomial Setting}
\small
A random variable $X$ follows a \textbf{binomial distribution} if all four
\textbf{BINS} conditions are met:

\vspace{8pt}
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{2.0}
\begin{tabularx}{\linewidth}{@{} >{\bfseries\color{navy}}p{0.06\linewidth}
                                     >{\bfseries}p{0.28\linewidth} X @{}}
B & Binary outcomes &
  Each trial has exactly \blank{3cm} outcomes: \blank{2cm} or \blank{2cm}. \\
I & Independent trials &
  The outcome of one trial \blank{4cm} another trial.
  Use the \blank{2cm} condition when sampling without replacement. \\
N & Fixed number of trials &
  The number of trials $n$ is \blank{4cm} in advance. \\
S & Same probability &
  The probability of success $p$ is \blank{4cm} on every trial. \\
\end{tabularx}

\vspace{8pt}
If all four conditions are met, write: $X \sim B(\blank{1.5cm},\;\blank{1.5cm})$
\end{notesbox}

\vspace{0.1in}

\begin{notesbox}{The Binomial Probability Formula}
\small

For $X \sim B(n, p)$, the probability of exactly $k$ successes is:
\[
  P(X = k) \;=\; \binom{n}{k}\,p^k\,(1-p)^{n-k}
\]

\vspace{4pt}
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.8}
\begin{tabularx}{\linewidth}{@{} >{\bfseries}p{0.22\linewidth} X @{}}
$\binom{n}{k} = \dfrac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}$ & counts the number of \blank{5cm}
  in which $k$ successes can occur among $n$ trials. \\
$p^k$ & the probability of \blank{5cm} in exactly $k$ trials. \\
$(1-p)^{n-k}$ & the probability of \blank{5cm} in the remaining $n-k$ trials. \\
\end{tabularx}

\vspace{8pt}
\textbf{Calculator:} \texttt{binompdf(}$n$\texttt{,}\;$p$\texttt{,}\;$k$\texttt{)}
gives $P(X = k)$ directly.

\vspace{8pt}
\textbf{AP Exam reminder:} Always show the formula setup even if you use the
calculator. Write $\binom{n}{k}p^k(1-p)^{n-k}$ with numbers substituted before
computing.
\end{notesbox}

\begin{notesbox}{Worked Example --- Random Guessing}
\small
\textbf{Context:} 10-question quiz, 4 choices per question, student guesses randomly.\\
$n = \blank{1.5cm}$, \quad $p = \blank{1.5cm}$, \quad success $= $ \blank{3cm}

\vspace{6pt}
\textbf{Check BINS:}\\[4pt]
B: \blank{10cm}\\[4pt]
I: \blank{10cm}\\[4pt]
N: \blank{10cm}\\[4pt]
S: \blank{10cm}

\vspace{6pt}
$X \sim B(\blank{1.5cm},\;\blank{1.5cm})$

\vspace{8pt}
\textbf{Compute} $P(X = 6)$:
\[
  P(X=6) \;=\; \binom{\blank{1cm}}{\blank{1cm}}\,(\blank{2cm})^6\,(\blank{2cm})^4
             \;=\; \blank{3cm} \;\approx\; \blank{2cm}
\]
Calculator check: \texttt{binompdf(}\blank{1cm}\texttt{,}\;\blank{1cm}\texttt{,}\;\blank{1cm}\texttt{)}
$= $ \blank{2cm}
\end{notesbox}
