\textbf{Identify each sampling method and answer the question.}

\vspace{8pt}
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em, itemsep=10pt]
  \item A state health department has a list of all 50{,}000 licensed drivers. A
        researcher picks the number 7 at random and then surveys every 500th driver
        on the list.

        Method: \blank{8cm}

  \item A hospital divides its 300 nurses into three departments: ICU, general ward,
        and emergency. The researcher randomly selects 15 nurses from each department.

        Method: \blank{8cm}

  \item A researcher wants to study commuter habits. She randomly selects 5 of the
        city's 40 bus routes and surveys every passenger on those routes on a Tuesday.

        Method: \blank{8cm}

  \item A researcher selects 5 names at random from a list of all 5{,}000 members of
        a professional organization.

        Method: \blank{8cm}

  \item For question 2, are the departments strata or clusters? How do you know?\\
        \writeline\\[1pt]\writeline
\end{enumerate}
