\begin{scenariobox}[Context]{sky}
\small
A school counselor randomly selects 60 students from all students in the district and
randomly assigns them to either a mindfulness program or a study hall period. After
8 weeks, the mindfulness group has significantly lower stress scores.
\end{scenariobox}

\vspace{0.1in}
