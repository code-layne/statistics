\begin{enumerate}

  \item \ans{Mode --- it records category labels, not numbers.}

  \item \ans{Reasonable center, but the spread (18 to 61 min) means the average may not tell the whole story.}

  \item \ans{Debatable. The gaps between scale values may not be equal, so the average may not be meaningful. Context matters.}

\begin{teachernote}
